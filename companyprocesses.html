Corporate English The Passive Voice

📌 Grammar Notes: Formality & Focus

We use the Passive Voice when the object is more important than the subject, when the subject is irrelevant/obvious, or simply to sound more formal in business.

  • Structure: Object + TO BE (gives the time/tense) + PARTICIPLE + complements
  • Present: "The salaries are paid on the 25th of each month."
  • Past: "Fifty packages were delivered last week."
  • Modals: "The money should be transferred in two workdays."
