📌 Grammar Notes: Structuring a Failure Story

  • Context (The Background): Use the Past Progressive (was/were + -ing).
    "I was organizing an important virtual event..."
  • The Problem (The Action): Use the Past Simple (-ed / irregular).
    "...and I sent the wrong meeting link."
  • Linking Words: Connect your ideas!
    • and (addition)
    • but / however / though (contrast, showing the problem)
    • so (result, introducing the lesson)
  • The Lesson: Use present or future to show growth.
    "...so next time, I will double-check all calendar invites."
