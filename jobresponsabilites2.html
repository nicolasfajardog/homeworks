📌 Grammar Notes: Describing your role

Pay close attention to the prepositions and verbs we use to describe our jobs naturally.

  • Position & Employer:
    "I work as an Industrial Engineer." (Profession)
    "I work for a company in the oil and gas industry." (Employer/Industry)
  • Responsibilities (-ing vs Base Form):
    "I am responsible for paying the custom duties." (Prep + -ing)
    "I am in charge of managing the freight." (Prep + -ing)
    "I have to stay updated on trade policies." (Infinitive to + base verb)
