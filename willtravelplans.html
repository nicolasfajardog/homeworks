📌 Grammar Reference – Future with WILL & Prepositions

Use will + verb for future plans and schedules. The negative is won't (will not).

✐️ I will fly to Miami. · The flight will depart at 6:00.

✐️ Will it be on time? → Yes, it will. / No, it won't.

✈️ Travel Prepositions:

  • FROM: Origin (Where does it start?) → The flight is from Bogotá.
  • TO: Destination (Where does it go?) → I will fly to Madrid.
  • WITH: Companions, luggage, or airlines → I travel with my family / with Avianca / with 2 bags.
  • AT: Specific locations or times → Board the plane at Gate 12. / It lands at 9:00.

🧳 Vocabulary – The Airport

Verbs: board (subirse), fly, land (aterrizar), take off (despegar), depart (salir), arrive (llegar).

Nouns: flight, arrivals (llegadas), departures (salidas), gate (puerta de embarque).

Luggage: baggage / bags (equipaje), suitcase (maleta de ruedas), carry-on (maleta de mano pequeña), purse (bolso), backpack (mochila).

Status: on time (a tiempo), delayed (retrasado), cancelled (cancelado).
