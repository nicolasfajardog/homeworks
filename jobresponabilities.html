📌 Grammar Notes: Responsibilities & Tenses

1. Expressing Duties:

  • Responsible for + (Verb-ing): "I am responsible for managing the team."
  • Have to + (Base Verb): "I have to call clients every day."

2. Present & Past Tenses:

  • Present Simple: Routines & facts. (I work in an office)
  • Present Progressive: Happening right now. (I am designing an app this month)
  • Past Simple: Finished past actions. (I worked at a bank in 2021)
  • Past Progressive: Actions in progress in the past. (I was studying while I was working)
