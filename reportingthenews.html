Grammar Tense Backshifting

📌 Grammar Notes: Reporting Verbs & Tenses

When retelling news, we use reporting verbs like said, told, reported, announced, and claimed. Remember to shift the tense backwards:

  • Simple Present → Simple Past ("I want..." → said he wanted)
  • Present Progressive → Past Progressive ("We are designing..." → told that they were designing)
  • Simple Past → Past Perfect ("She sang..." → claimed she had sung)
  • Present Perfect → Past Perfect ("They haven't won..." → reported they hadn't won)
  • Will → Would ("We will win..." → announced they would win)
